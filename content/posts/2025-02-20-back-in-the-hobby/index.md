Back in 2003, when I was in secondary school, I got my first Warhammer Fantasy miniatures and started collecting High Elves. There were no Youtube tutorials and my source of tips was exclusively the instructions in the box.

Once I went to University, I decided to pretend I wasn't a massive nerd and just abandoned the hobby.

Fast forward almost 20 years and I'm back. Things have changed: developed patience, endless sources of information and disposable income.

In this blog I want to document my hobby journey and progress. Here's a High Elf spearmen champion I painted around when I started.
